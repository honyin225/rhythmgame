ℹ️ GAME INFO

🎮 HOW TO PLAY:

1. Click 🎤 MIC OFF to enable microphone
2. Press RECORD and tap each red button (1-4)
3. Play that note on your instrument to record it
4. Buttons turn GREEN when recorded
5. Press START and play along with the notes!

🎵 GAME MODES:

• NORMAL MODE: Endless play with progress bar, no time limit
• TIME MODE: 60-second countdown, to get high score!
• SURVIVAL MODE: 10 hearts, MISSES cost hearts


⚡ CHALLENGES:

• 🌱 EASY: All long notes, very slow
• 🌿 MEDIUM: All long notes, fast
• 🔥 HARD: All short notes, fast
• ⚡ EXPERT: Mixed long/short, fast

🎯 HIT ZONES:

• GOOD : +150 points
• GREAT : +100 points
• MISS : Combo will recount if miss


🏆 RANKING SYSTEM:

• 3 ranking for NORMAL, TIME, and SURVIVAL modes
• Top 10 scores are saved locally per mode
• Enter your name when you get a high score

🎯 AIMS OF THE GAME:

• To discover whether the gamification experience could enhance motivation to practice instrument
• three game features:
• a) scores/rewards system
• b) immersive display,
• c) connection with authentic instrument.


💡 Capstone Project

This game is the complete version.
Prediction: This complete design will enhance motivation of music practices to a greater extent, compared to incomplete version
